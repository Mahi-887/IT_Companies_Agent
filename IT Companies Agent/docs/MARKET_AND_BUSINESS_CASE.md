# Market and Business Case

## Core Market Pain

Across IT companies, the biggest repeatable engineering pain is internal knowledge debt. Teams lose time because system understanding is fragmented across code, docs, tickets, and people.

## Why This Problem Matters

- Every new engineer needs days or weeks to understand the codebase
- Senior engineers become knowledge bottlenecks
- Documentation is usually outdated
- Team velocity drops when context is trapped inside a few people

## DevPulse AI Value

DevPulse AI converts internal engineering knowledge into a searchable and explainable backend system.

## Buyer Value

- Lower onboarding cost
- Faster first contribution from new developers
- Better continuity when senior engineers switch teams
- Less time wasted in Slack and repeated calls

## Team Value

- Developers ask the system before interrupting seniors
- Leads get module and ownership visibility
- Support and QA teams get faster debugging context

## Product Differentiation

- Focused on engineering knowledge, not generic chat
- Combines code, docs, tickets, Slack, and logs
- Returns references, not only summaries
- Designed for local-first and minimal backend deployment

## Commercial Angle

This can be sold as a SaaS workflow platform for engineering teams, or deployed as a company-hosted internal tool for regulated teams that need tighter data control.

## Expansion Path

- Multi-repo support
- Team knowledge analytics
- AI-driven engineering runbooks
- Release-risk and ownership insights
